Hope you all had a present from Father Christmas!
I had 2 presents from him. The gifts are:
I also ate my favourite food: Chicken Biriyani made by my mom.
I helped her too in preparing. The recipe is:
  1. Marinate Chicken with chilli powder, salt, yogurt and ginger garlic paste and leave it in the fridge for 30 mins.
  2. Heat oil in a pressure cooker and add bay leaves, cinnamon and cloves.
  3. Add finely chopped onions and green chillies and saute for 5 mins.
  4. Add ginger garlic paste and saute for 5 mins.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes and saute for 5 mins.
  6. Add finely chopped coriander and mint leaves and saute for 5 mins.
  7. Add the marinated chicken from the fridge and let is cook.
  8. Whatever many cups of rice you take, double the cups of water.
  9. Taste.
  10. Let it cook for according to your taste.
  11. Serve and eat!
