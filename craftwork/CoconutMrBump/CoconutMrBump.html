How to make a Mr. Bump out of Coconut

You'll need:

  • Coconut Shell
  • Sandpaper
  • Acrylic Paints: Blue & White
  • Gel Pens

How to make a bendy snake.

  1. Take a Coconut Shell and shine it with a Sandpaper.

  2. Paint it with Acrylic Paints.You can see a pic if you need help.

  3. Draw eyes & mouth with Gel Pens.


Now your Coconut Mr. Bump is ready!
